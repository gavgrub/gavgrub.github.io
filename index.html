About Me

Hello, I'm Gavin. I am a student from Canada studying computer science and mathematics at the University of Calgary. There, I take courses in a number of subjects such as programming, discrete math, data structures, statistics, and calculus. I aspire to develop my skills to pursue a career in computer graphics or software engineering.

I work for the University of Calgary Dinos, where I act in a number of roles. These include gate check: selling and scanning tickets, access control: restricting access to key locations, and concession: preparing food and operating point of sale systems.

Outside of my academic and career-oriented interests, I enjoy music, history, and gaming. These activities help me recharge and refocus on my professional goals.
